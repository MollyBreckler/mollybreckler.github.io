I picked the text 'The First Thousand Numbers Classified In Alphabetical Order' by Claude Closky. This archive stresses the anal nature of the English word through its constant repitition and time consuming qualities. Just like the constant advancement of technology and the digital age, I looked at addressing how today it is extremley common to exchange the written form of a letter for the numerical version. Rather then writing out the tedious English language of for example, a three worded letter, people now turn to numbers. Originally the text is quite long and therefore, can easily be shortened through the expression of numbers.

I have reposted a section of the text into instagram to explore the notion of numbers and the ease to manipulate and shorten things. I created 1 account where I posted a small section of the text with numbers. Making them all look the same, however, slightly altering the hashtags. Then I created a second instagram page, called the tester, who only followed the instagram page 'First Thousand Numbers'.

Instagram have updated so that rather then showing images based on the time they were uploaded, it is now based on populairty and likes. Hence, uploading all images at the same time, and having a public account the 'Tester'account would show a numerical pattern from the number of likes each image receives. Creating a complete number solution rather than the original alphabetical text.
