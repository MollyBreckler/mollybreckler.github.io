Claude Closky the creator of the text 'The first thousand numbers classified in alphabetical order' was a French man. English was not his first language, however, he wrote this text which is heavily based around the English language. After some further research some believe that the order/ the way it was written out was actually incorrect and there are some mistakes - basing all of this around the fact that Closky's first language was not English. For this outcome, I wanted to play against the orginal interpretation of this text, which was to be expressed in English and repost it in French. Doing this by using SoundCloud, considering making a voice memo or taking a voice over from a langauge aid app and basically speaking the text in French.
