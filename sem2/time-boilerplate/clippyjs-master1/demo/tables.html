Procrastination is the avoidance of doing a task that needs to be accomplished.

Sometimes, procrastination takes place until the "last minute" before a deadline.

Procrastination can take hold on any aspect of life—putting off cleaning the stove,

repairing a leaky roof, seeing a doctor or dentist, submitting a job report

or academic assignment or broaching a stressful issue with a partner.

Procrastination can lead to feelings of guilt, inadequacy, depression and self-doubt.
